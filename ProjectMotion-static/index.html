Project Motion

Who are we?

Project Motion is a first of its kind for Cape Town and South Africa.
It is an indoor venue housing various features and facilities associated with non-motorised action sports. Customers will be able to take part in action sports such as BMX, skateboarding, competitive climbing and rollerblading amongst others.
